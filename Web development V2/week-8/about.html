We’re a small team that cares about the craft

Nova Studio was founded on a simple belief: better websites come from tight collaboration between designers and developers. We work in small, focused squads to keep communication clear and momentum high. Our process is transparent, our feedback is candid, and our standards are high—because your users expect nothing less.

We’ve shipped dozens of projects across B2B SaaS, e-commerce, education, and creator brands. While the industries vary, our playbook stays consistent: understand the real problem, design for clarity, and build with performance and accessibility in mind.

  • Accessibility-first: semantic HTML, keyboard support, and inclusive content.
  • Performance budgets: set limits early; ship fast experiences.
  • Design systems: tokens and components for scalable UI.
  • Async by default: clear docs and short, efficient calls.

How we collaborate

You’ll get a single point of contact, weekly progress notes, and staged previews of your site. We use structured feedback rounds so everyone stays aligned and decisions are documented.

Toolbox

  • Figma for design and prototyping
  • GitHub for version control and issues
  • Vercel/Netlify for fast, reliable hosting
  • Analytics for honest, data-informed iteration

Values that guide our work

Clarity

We avoid jargon and focus on what users need to know to act. Clear communication reduces risk and speeds up delivery.

Craft

Details matter: micro-interactions, load order, focus states, and empty states. They’re small on their own but huge when combined.

Care

We make choices that respect your users’ time, attention, and privacy. Long-term trust beats short-term tricks every time.

We craft fast, accessible websites and delightful digital products.
